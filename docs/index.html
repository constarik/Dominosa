How to Play

1

Look at the grid

The grid is filled with numbers. Hidden beneath are dominoes — each covering exactly two adjacent cells.

2

Pair adjacent cells

Tap a cell, then tap its neighbor to form a domino pair. Or swipe between them on mobile.

3

Use every domino exactly once

Each number combination (like 2•5) appears exactly once. Duplicates light up red. Fill the whole grid to win.

Choose Your Challenge

Easy

Medium
